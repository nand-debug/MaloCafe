Our Story

A little corner of warmth in the heart of Suva

From a Small Idea to a Local Favourite

Malo Cafe began with a simple goal: to create a welcoming place in Suva where people could slow down, share a meal, and enjoy a good cup of coffee. Located on Grantham Road, we set out to build something warm, relaxed, and real.

What started as a small café has grown into a go-to spot for brunch and coffee lovers. We’ve kept it at heart: good ingredients, honest food, and a team that genuinely cares about the people who walk through our doors.

Whether you’re studying, catching up with friends, or just stopping by for your usual coffee, Malo Cafe is a place where you can always feel comfortable coming back to.
