Our Story

A little corner of warmth in the heart of Suva

From a Simple Dream to Suva's Favourite Brunch Spot

Malo Cafe started with a simple idea: create a space where friends, families, and strangers could come together over great food and even better coffee. Nestled on Grantham Road in Suva, we opened our doors with a commitment to quality, warmth, and genuine hospitality.

What began as a small coffee spot quickly grew into one of Suva's most beloved brunch destinations. Our secret? We keep things simple — fresh ingredients, recipes made with care, and a team that treats every guest like family.

Whether you're a student looking for a quiet study corner, a group of friends catching up over pancakes, or a coffee lover searching for the perfect latte — Malo Cafe is your place.
